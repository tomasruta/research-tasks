 Where conversations win. 

Intake & Onboarding 

Intake and onboarding are about capturing the essential information that gets new clients, patients, or users started smoothly. Traditional forms overwhelm people with rigid fields and high drop-off rates. 

RowFlow makes the process easy by guiding them through a friendly conversation that collects everything you need, asks smart clarifying questions, and keeps completion rates high so setup feels seamless instead of bureaucratic. 

Customer Feedback 

Customer feedback is about more than scores - it’s about understanding the “why” behind them so you can take meaningful action. Traditional surveys collect numbers but miss context, leaving you guessing. 
With RowFlow, a natural conversation captures both the rating and the story, digging deeper when something matters so you get insights your team can actually use. 

Lead Qualification Forms 

Lead qualification is about quickly understanding who’s a real prospect and whether they’re worth your team’s time. Traditional long forms ask for too much too soon, causing drop-offs and giving you shallow data at best. 

With RowFlow, a short conversation uncovers the real pain points, qualifies intent, and smoothly books a call so you get better leads without scaring them away. 

Surveys & Research 

Surveys and research are meant to uncover insights that guide decisions, but traditional forms reduce responses to rigid scales and often miss the deeper context. 

RowFlow turns surveys into natural conversations that keep people engaged, ask clarifying follow-ups, and capture both quantitative data and the stories behind it — giving you richer insights to drive real action. 

Internal Check-Ins 

Most teams rely on trackers or update templates — spreadsheets, project tools, or Confluence pages — but they often get ignored or filled with vague one-liners. The result is leaders chasing updates and teams treating them as a chore. 

RowFlow makes check-ins effortless through a quick conversation that captures clear progress, asks smart follow-ups, and turns updates into structured reports without the overhead.
