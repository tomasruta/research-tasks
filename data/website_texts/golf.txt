Security and compliance built in 

Everything enterprises need to deploy agents safely — threat protection, access control, and audit trails. 

Stop threats before they spread 

Agents can leak data, execute indirect prompt injections, or expose sensitive info without anyone noticing. Golf scans every interaction in real-time — blocking attacks and redacting PII before they reach your systems. 

Know who did what 

Shared API keys make it impossible to trace actions back to a person. Golf ties every agent action to a real identity through Okta, Azure, or your IdP — so you can enforce access policies per user, per tool. 

Shared API keys make it impossible to trace actions back to a person. Golf ties every agent action to a real identity through Okta, Azure, or your IdP — so you can enforce access policies per user, per tool. 

Audit-ready from day one 

When compliance asks what happened, you need answers fast. Golf streams structured logs to Sentinel, Splunk, or your SIEM — giving you full data lineage and SOC 2 readiness out of the box.
