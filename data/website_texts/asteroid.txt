Got questions? + 

 Who is Asteroid for? + 

Asteroid is built for both non-technical teams and engineers who need to automate complex browser workflows. It's used by product managers overseeing inefficient internal processes, heads of operations looking to eliminate repetitive manual tasks, and executives driving large-scale automation across their organizations. 
 What kind of tasks can I automate? + 

Asteroid automates complex browser tasks like data retrieval and intelligent entry of structured data into legacy systems—especially where no APIs exist and traditional tools fall short.

Real-world use cases include insurance quoting across provider portals, patient data entry into healthcare systems, invoice retrieval from supplier portals, reporting to Google Sheets, large-scale grant applications, and connecting voice agents to legacy workflows 
 Can you build an agent for me? + 

Yes. Book a demo and tell us about your use case—we'll scope it with you and build a custom browser agent, typically within days to weeks. 
 Can I customise and supervise my agents? + 

Yes. You have full control—build agents in the platform, customize their logic, and supervise them in real time. 
 Do I need to write complex code? + 

No.

We believe domain experts—not just developers—should be able to build and manage automation directly. Whether you're pulling quotes across insurer portals, integrating voice agents with legacy systems, or scaling back-office operations, Asteroid gives you the power to move fast—without relying on fragile RPA or slow custom development.
