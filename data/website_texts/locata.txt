How It Works 
Not a platform or a tool - complete workflow automation 

 Step 1 
Setup & Integration 
Connect your EHR and payor portal credentials to Locata. Our platform handles the technical setup, ensuring secure and compliant data exchange. 

 Step 2 
Automated Processing 
Our proprietary AI identifies patterns from your historical referrals to learn how your team operates. We then execute on your behalf, automating the workflow at every step. 

 Step 3 
Referral Tracking 
Monitor the status of every referral in real-time. Our platform provides automated updates and alerts, ensuring your team receives the latest status of each referral. 

 Step 4 
Patient Engagement 
Keep patients informed with automated notifications about their referrals. Leverage Voice AI to increase engagement with patients. 

 Step 5 
Results & Follow-up 
Automatically request consultation notes and follow-up reports from specialists, effortlessly closing the referral loop.
