Cosmic is the only platform that integrates everything you need in one place. No need for separate services for authentication, databases, payments, or hosting. 
For example, with other platforms, you would need to manage your own Stripe account to charge users. With Cosmic, you can just use Cosmic Payments with zero initial setup.
