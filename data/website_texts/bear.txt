Frequently asked questions. 
Everything you need to know about Bear and GEO. 

 What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)? + 

GEO is the practice of optimizing your brand's visibility in AI-powered search engines and assistants like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Claude, and Perplexity. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in Google's blue links, GEO ensures AI agents accurately recommend and cite your brand when users ask relevant questions. 
 How does Bear track AI mentions of my brand? + 

Bear monitors responses from major AI platforms — including ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity — for mentions of your brand across thousands of relevant prompts. You get a real-time dashboard showing when, where, and how AI agents talk about you. 
 How is GEO different from traditional SEO? + 

SEO optimizes for search engine result pages (SERPs) and link-based ranking. GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers where there are no links to click — the AI either recommends you or it doesn't. Bear helps you influence what AI agents say about your brand by optimizing your content, building citations on third-party sources, and tracking your visibility across AI platforms. 
 Which AI platforms does Bear monitor? + 

Bear tracks GPT-5, Google AI Mode, Google AI Overviews, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. We're constantly adding new platforms as the AI search landscape evolves. 
 How long does it take to see results? + 

Most customers see measurable improvements in AI visibility within 4–6 weeks. Bear's blog agent and PR outreach tools start building AI-optimized content and third-party citations immediately, and our analytics dashboard lets you track progress from day one. 
 What does the blog agent do? + 

Bear's blog agent generates structured, AI-ready content designed to be easily ingested and cited by LLMs. It creates blog posts optimized for the prompts your target audience is asking AI agents, helping your brand surface in AI-generated recommendations.
