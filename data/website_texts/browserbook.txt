 Why BrowserBook ? 

Web automation is hard. BrowserBook makes it easy. 
Scripts are deterministic but brittle; agents are flexible but unreliable and prone to prompt injection attacks. 
BrowserBook delivers speed, stability, and savings with an AI-powered IDE for building, maintaining, and deploying deterministic automations. 

Deterministic by default. 
Use AI to write code, not stumble through your workflow. Build fast, reliable, automations that run 10x cheaper than browser agents. 

Build, test, and ship faster. 
Context-aware AI enables you to automate in a fraction of the time. Move from idea to production workflow in minutes, not weeks. 

Authentication made simple. 
Use our secure authentication framework to safely store and manage credentials for authenticated workflows. 

Seamless API integration. 
Trigger workflows via API and leverage global proxies to bypass bot detection, ensuring automations remain reliable at scale.
